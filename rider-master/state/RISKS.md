# RISKS — things that could go wrong later

> Reviewed at every audit (P09) and every re-plan (P03). A risk that
> materialized becomes a task/debug entry; a mitigated risk stays as history.
> Prompts for finding them: long-lead parts? single points of failure?
> unproven algorithms? deadline collisions? dependence on one person/machine/
> access? disabled safety interlocks?

Format per entry:

```
## RISK-### — <what could happen>
- Status: open | mitigating | materialized | closed
- Raised: YYYY-MM-DD · Severity: low/med/high · Likelihood: low/med/high
- Impact if it hits: <what breaks, what it costs>
- Early warning sign: <what to watch for>
- Mitigation / fallback: <action taken up-front, or plan B>
```
